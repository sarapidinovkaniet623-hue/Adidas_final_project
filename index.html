The History of Adidas

Adidas was founded in Germany in 1949 by Adolf
Dassler, who was known by the nickname Adi. He
started producing high-quality sports shoes, and the
companys name was created from his first and last
names. Over time, Adidas became one of the worlds
leading brands for sportswear and footwear.
